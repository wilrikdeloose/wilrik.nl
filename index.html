Wilrik

Born and raised in 1983, Eindhoven, The Netherlands. Hi, I'm Wilrik. I studied at the Fontys Hogeschool of ICT (FHICT) where I got my Bachelor's degree. After my graduation I got my Master's degree in Computer Science at the Technical University of Eindhoven (TU/e). I started working as a software engineer at different companies. Finally, I made a carreer switch and became an ICT teacher at FHICT.
